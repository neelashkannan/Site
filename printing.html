Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats do you accept?

+

We accept most standard 3D file formats including STL, OBJ, STEP, IGES, and more. For best results, we recommend using STL files with appropriate resolution for your application.

How long does it take to print my order?

+

Production times vary based on complexity, size, material, and current production volume. Standard orders typically take 3-5 business days, while rush services can deliver in as little as 24 hours for qualifying projects. You'll receive a specific timeline with your quote.

What is your minimum order quantity?

+

We have no minimum order quantity. We're happy to print single prototypes or larger production runs based on your needs.

Can you help optimize my design for 3D printing?

+

Yes! Our design team can review your files and suggest modifications to improve printability, reduce costs, or enhance performance. This service is complimentary for most orders or available as a standalone service for complex projects.

Do you offer custom finishes and colors?

+

We offer a variety of finishing options including sanding, polishing, painting, and specialty coatings. Color options depend on the selected material, with some materials offering a wider range than others. Custom color matching is available for certain materials and larger orders.
