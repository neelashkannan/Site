Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a quote for my 3D printing project?

+

You can request a quote by filling out our contact form above, emailing us directly at info@robonium.tech, or calling our office during business hours. For the most accurate quote, please include details about your project requirements, material preferences, and any deadlines.

What information should I provide when requesting a quote?

+

To provide the most accurate quote, please include: your 3D model file (if available), quantity needed, material preferences, desired finish, dimensional requirements, timeline, and any specific functional requirements. The more details you provide, the more precise our quote will be.

Can I visit your facility to discuss my project in person?

+

Yes! We welcome visitors to our Coimbatore facility. Please contact us in advance to schedule an appointment so we can ensure the appropriate team members are available to discuss your specific project needs.

Do you offer rush services for urgent projects?

+

Yes, we offer rush services for projects with tight deadlines. Depending on current production volumes and the complexity of your project, we can often accommodate expedited timelines. Rush services may incur additional fees, which will be clearly outlined in your quote.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. For larger projects, we typically require a 50% deposit to begin production, with the remaining balance due upon completion before shipping.
