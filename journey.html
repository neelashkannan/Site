Our Journey

From campus innovation to technological revolution

Our story begins at Sri Krishna College of Engineering and Technology, where two Mechatronics students with a passion for innovation identified a fundamental problem: talented students were compromising their designs due to limited fabrication resources.

With our personal 3D printer, we began offering printing services at cost price - charging only for the filament used. Our goal wasn't profit; it was empowering genuine innovators who previously couldn't afford to bring their ideas to life.

As word spread, project quality improved and innovation flourished. This grassroots movement caught the attention of local startups and incubation centers, who saw in our work a solution to their own prototyping challenges.

Our Vision

Inspired by Japan's transformation, where innovation thrives with approximately one business for every 40-50 people, we're making advanced manufacturing and robotics accessible to everyone in India.

Today, we're expanding beyond 3D printing into robotics and automation - not to replace human workers, but to create new opportunities for innovation and entrepreneurship. We're building a future where the only limit to innovation is imagination, not access to technology.
