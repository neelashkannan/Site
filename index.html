Strong & Abrasion Resistant

Extremely tough, durable, and versatile with excellent abrasion resistance. Ideal for functional parts like gears, hinges, and living hinges.

  • Resolution: ¬±0.15mm - ¬±0.25mm
  • Strength: Very High
  • Heat Resistance: High (approx. 120-150¬∞C, varies by type)

Rubber-like Elasticity

Thermoplastic Polyurethane (TPU) is a flexible, rubber-like material. Excellent for parts requiring elasticity, impact absorption, or vibration dampening.

  • Resolution: ¬±0.2mm - ¬±0.3mm
  • Flexibility: High (Shore A hardness varies)
  • Durability: Good abrasion resistance
  • Colors: Multiple options
  • Best for: Seals, grips, flexible joints

Ultra-High Detail

Resin-based printing (SLA/DLP) offers exceptional detail, smooth surface finish, and dimensional accuracy. Ideal for intricate models, jewelry, and dental applications.

  • Resolution: ¬±0.025mm - ¬±0.05mm
  • Strength: Varies (Standard, Tough, Castable, etc.)
  • Detail: Very High
  • Colors: Various, including clear & colored
  • Best for: Miniatures, molds, dental models
