Tips for Better Prints

  • Ensure your model has proper wall thickness (min 1mm for most materials)
  • Check for manifold errors or non-watertight meshes
  • Consider the orientation of the model for strength and surface finish
  • Add support structures for overhangs greater than 45 degrees if needed
  • Include details about critical dimensions or tolerances in your notes
